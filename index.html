Upskill for Free (Certifications)

Access university-level courses. Tip: Choose the "Audit" option to learn the material entirely for free.

LinkedIn Learning

Give Back & Grow

Volunteering isn't just about giving back; it's a massive resume booster. It demonstrates leadership, teamwork, empathy, and initiative.

Skills-Based Volunteering

Websites like Catchafire match your specific professional skills (graphic design, writing, coding, accounting) with non-profits that need online help.

Local Impact

Use VolunteerMatch or Idealist to find local community centers, animal shelters, or food banks in your hometown.

Future-Proof Your Skills (Tech & AI)

AI & Prompt Engineering

No matter your major, knowing how to effectively prompt AI tools (like ChatGPT) is becoming a critical workplace skill for boosting productivity.

"Build Your Own X"

A massive GitHub repository filled with step-by-step guides to recreating your favorite technologies (bots, games, web servers) from scratch.

FreeCodeCamp

Learn HTML, CSS, and JS to build websites and write scripts. Coding logic is a fantastic problem-solving skill to add to your resume.

Google Digital Garage

Free tutorials on digital marketing basics, data analytics, and understanding how the modern web works.

The "You" Project

How to Start a Personal Project

A personal project is the ultimate proof that you are a self-starter. Here is your roadmap:

Step 1: Find the Spark

What bothers you? What do you love? Find a problem you want to solve or a topic you want to master.

Step 2: Define the Output

Make it tangible. Will it be a blog, a coded app, a business plan, a piece of research, or a YouTube video?

Step 3: Set a Timeline

Treat it like a job. Dedicate specific hours every week. Set 8 weekly milestones for the summer.

Step 4: Document It

Build in public. Post weekly updates on LinkedIn or create a simple portfolio website to show your progress.
